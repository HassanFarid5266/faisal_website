This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Faisal Abbas Facility Management Services uses them on our website (faisalabbas.pk), and what choices you have about their use. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide useful information to website owners.

Cookies do not contain personally identifying information on their own, but they may be linked to personal information we hold about you. They are not harmful to your device and cannot access other files or data stored on it.

Similar technologies — including local storage, session storage and web beacons — may also be used to achieve similar purposes. For simplicity, we refer to all of these as "cookies" throughout this policy.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services that appear on our pages. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies, and we recommend you review them:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery. No personally identifiable data is stored.
  • WhatsApp / social media widgets — if you use share or chat buttons, those platforms may set their own cookies.

We do not have control over third-party cookies and cannot access the data they collect. We carefully select our third-party providers and limit their use to services genuinely necessary for our website to function well.

5. Your Controls

5.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept all cookies or select essential cookies only. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

5.2 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View which cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block cookies from all or specific websites
  • Set preferences for future cookie handling

Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of our website from functioning correctly. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use the site.

5.3 Opt-Out of Analytics

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking specifically, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Your Consent

By continuing to use our website after seeing the cookie banner, or by clicking "Accept All", you consent to our use of non-essential cookies as described in this policy. Selecting "Essential Only" will disable analytics, functional and marketing cookies.

Your consent is valid for 12 months, after which we will ask again. You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time as our website evolves or regulations change. When we make significant changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you via the cookie banner on your next visit.
