This Privacy Policy explains how Faisal Abbas Facility Management Services ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares and protects your personal information when you visit our website or engage our services. Please read this carefully before providing us with your personal data.

1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Faisal Abbas Facility Management Services is a registered business operating out of Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan. This policy applies to all personal data we collect through our website (faisalabbas.pk), our contact forms, phone calls, email correspondence and during the provision of our services.

By using our website or services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of it, please do not use our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Full name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Company or property name and address
  • Service requirements and project details shared through our contact form or by phone
  • Correspondence records including emails and messages
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely through third-party providers)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring URLs and device type
  • Cookie identifiers (see Section 6)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide quotations for our services
  • To deliver and manage the facility management services you have contracted us for
  • To process payments and maintain financial records
  • To send service updates, appointment confirmations and relevant communications
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors use it
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations under Pakistani law
  • To detect, investigate and prevent fraud or other illegal activities

We will only use your data in ways that are compatible with the purposes for which it was collected. We will not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service delivery partners: When we engage subcontractors or specialist teams to fulfil a service on your behalf, we share only the information they need to do so.
  • Payment processors: Your billing information is processed by our trusted payment partners using industry-standard encryption.
  • Legal obligations: We may disclose your information if required by Pakistani law, court order or government authority.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity under equivalent privacy protections.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • Restricted access to personal data within our organisation (need-to-know basis)
  • Regular review and update of our security practices
  • Secure disposal of physical and electronic records when no longer needed

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you promptly in the event of a data breach that affects your personal data.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files placed on your device — to improve your browsing experience. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly (e.g. navigation preferences).
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it.
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences for a better experience on return visits.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data where it is no longer necessary for the purpose it was collected.
  • Objection: Object to our use of your data for direct marketing purposes.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@faisalabbas.pk. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Specifically:

  • Enquiry data from non-clients: retained for up to 12 months
  • Active client records: retained for the duration of the contract plus 7 years (for financial compliance)
  • Website analytics data: retained for up to 24 months in aggregated form

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify existing clients directly. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
