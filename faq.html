General
Faisal Abbas is Pakistan's leading facility management company based in Lahore. Since 2010, we have been providing comprehensive workspace solutions including cleaning, security, parking management, civil works, landscaping and full office management for residential, commercial and industrial clients across Pakistan.
We are headquartered in Lahore and operate across all major cities in Pakistan including Karachi, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan and Peshawar. For enquiries outside these cities, please contact us and we will confirm availability.
We were founded in 2010, giving us over 15 years of experience in facility management. Over this time we have completed 500+ projects and currently serve 200+ active clients across Pakistan's residential, commercial and industrial sectors.
Yes. Every member of our team undergoes a thorough background check before being deployed on any client site. This includes identity verification, reference checks and police clearance for roles involving security or sensitive access. All staff also sign confidentiality agreements.
Services
We offer a full range of cleaning services including daily office cleaning, deep cleaning, post-construction cleaning, hospital and clinical cleaning (using bio-safe protocols), industrial facility cleaning, carpet and upholstery cleaning, and outdoor/facade cleaning. All services use certified eco-friendly products.
Yes. We offer uniformed, trained security services including guard deployment, access control, CCTV monitoring and visitor management. Our security staff are background-verified and trained to professional standards. Many clients take both cleaning and security as a bundled contract for better value.
Absolutely. We have experience managing landscaping for sites ranging from residential gardens to 40+ acre industrial facilities. Our landscaping services include grounds maintenance, seasonal planting, irrigation system installation and management, tree care and outdoor aesthetic design.
Our Office Management service covers organisational structuring, HR and staffing support, procurement management, internal communications frameworks, maintenance planning and compliance reporting. We essentially act as an embedded facility management partner, aligned with your operational goals.
Yes. Post-construction cleaning is one of our specialist services. We handle dust removal, debris clearance, window and glass cleaning, floor treatment and final deep-clean preparation to make a newly built or renovated space ready for occupancy. We work closely with construction teams to schedule appropriately.
Pricing & Contracts
Pricing is tailored to each client based on the scope of services, size of premises, frequency of service and contract duration. We provide a free, no-obligation quote after an initial consultation. Longer-term contracts typically offer better value per service. Contact us at our contact page to request a quote.
We offer flexible contracts to suit your needs: daily, weekly, monthly or annual. One-off projects are also available. Monthly contracts require 30 days' notice to cancel; annual contracts require 60 days' notice. We do not lock clients into unnecessary long-term commitments.
We do not publish a strict minimum, but our services are most cost-effective for premises requiring regular ongoing service. For very small one-off jobs, we will advise you honestly whether our service is the right fit. We believe in building long-term relationships, not single transactions.
We accept bank transfer (IBFT), cheque and online payment. Invoices are issued at the start of each billing period and are due within 14 days for monthly contracts, or as agreed for annual and project-based contracts. Payment details are provided in your Service Agreement.
Operations
For most services we can mobilise within 3–7 working days of signing a Service Agreement and receiving the required advance payment. For large or complex contracts requiring staff training, uniform preparation and equipment procurement, we typically allow 10–14 days for proper onboarding.
Our office hours are Monday to Saturday, 9am to 7pm. However, we offer 24/7 emergency response for existing contract clients who require urgent cleaning or maintenance support outside normal hours. Emergency availability is confirmed in your Service Agreement.
We take quality seriously. If you are not satisfied with any service visit, contact your dedicated account manager within 24 hours and we will rectify the issue at no additional cost. Persistent quality concerns are escalated to our Operations Head for a formal review and remediation plan.
Yes. Every client is assigned a dedicated Account Manager who is your single point of contact for scheduling, queries, invoicing and quality feedback. You will also have direct access to the on-site team supervisor during service hours.
Quality & Standards
Yes. We use certified eco-friendly cleaning products across all services wherever possible. For specialist environments like hospitals and food-processing facilities, we use appropriate bio-safe or industrial-grade products as required by international hygiene standards. Product safety data sheets are available on request.
Our cleaning and facility management processes are aligned with ISO hygiene and quality standards. We undergo regular internal audits and provide compliance reporting to clients who require it. For specific certification requirements on your project, please discuss this during your initial consultation.
Yes. Faisal Abbas carries professional indemnity and public liability insurance appropriate to the scale of services we provide. Insurance certificates are available to clients upon written request. We recommend that clients also maintain their own premises and contents insurance.
All staff receive structured induction training covering safety procedures, product handling, client interaction standards and sector-specific protocols (e.g. clinical environments, industrial hazards). We conduct refresher training quarterly and site-specific briefings before any new deployment. Our supervisors hold relevant professional qualifications.
