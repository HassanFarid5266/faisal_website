Our Story

15 Years of Excellence

Faisal Abbas was founded in 2010 with a single mission: to deliver world-class facility management services to the businesses and homes of Pakistan. What started as a small cleaning operation in Lahore has grown into a full-service office management company serving over 1,000 clients across Pakistan.

Over the years, we have expanded our services to include janitorial services, security, civil works, parking management, landscaping and complete office management — all under one roof.

Today, with a dedicated team of 460+ trained professionals, we continue to raise the bar for quality, reliability and customer satisfaction in Pakistan's facility management industry.
