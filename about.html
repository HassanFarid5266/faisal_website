How We Started

From One Office to Hundreds

Faisal Abbas was founded in Lahore in 2010 with a single mission: to give Pakistani businesses access to world-class facility management without the complexity or inflated costs that came with it.

What began as a small cleaning and maintenance operation has grown into Pakistan's most comprehensive office working solution — serving residential complexes, corporate towers, industrial plants, hospitals and hotels across the country.

Today, with over 200 active clients and 500+ completed projects, we remain committed to the same values that drove us from day one: reliability, quality and genuine care for the spaces we manage.
